About ASTU

Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU) is one of the leading higher education institutions in Ethiopia, focusing on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Located in Adama, the university is committed to providing quality education, fostering research, and contributing to the socio-economic development of the country.

Academic Programs

ASTU offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs in various fields of study, including:

  • Engineering
  • Computer Science
  • Natural Sciences
  • Business and Economics
  • Health Sciences

Admissions

Adama Science and Technology University welcomes students from all over the world. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Whether you are a prospective undergraduate or graduate student, we are here to help you every step of the way.

Research and Innovation

ASTU is at the forefront of research and innovation in Ethiopia. Our researchers are working on cutting-edge projects in areas such as renewable energy, artificial intelligence, and sustainable development. We collaborate with industry partners and international institutions to drive innovation and create real-world impact.
